Strategic Goals

Creating a work platform that includes the Engineering Encyclopedia Center and the website ,Meeting market needs for 3D printing in terms of design for medical issues ,Production of 3D printers Programming to improve the working systems of 3D printers at the programming level and at the hardware level (for example, maintenance).

Sub Objectives

Spreading the culture of 3D printing to help create a market for 3D printing. ,Assisting in the production of projects through 3D printing, where these projects respond to specific problems in the world and find solutions to them.

Procdural Objectives

Securing a stable income for the engineers participating in the work, and relying on the profit through the Internet in the first stage ,Finding a new source of income for all the encyclopedia workers in Syria and abroad.
